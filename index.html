    Hello!

    I am Sonu - a graduate student in Computer Engineering at Colorado State University. I am a graduate researcher at CSU Energy Institute working under the guidance of Dr. Ross Beveridge and Daniel Zimmerle. My current research involves applying computer vision, machine learning and big data techniques towards methane emission studies in Colorado, United States. My primary research interests are in 3D face reconstruction and human digitization. Previously I worked as a Research Engineer at Humanitarian technologies lab, India. I worked on developing a Gesture controlled wheelchair and few other robotics projects. Outside my engineering life, I like to play soccer, play piano and travel. Checkout some of my projects in the projects section. Feel free to send a hi!! at sonudileep@gmail.com if I can be of any help to you.
